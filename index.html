Since 2023

Two states on
one flat-top

TexAna started with a simple idea: you shouldn’t have to pick between a proper Texas taco and Louisiana’s love of anything fried, spiced, and shareable. So we put both on the truck.

We’re a small, women-owned local business — rolling through Gonzales, Prairieville and across Ascension Parish, setting up at RPCC, neighborhood pop-ups, farmers markets and private events. “The only thing different between us and a brick-and-mortar,” as our owner puts it, “is that we can travel to you.”

  • Women-owned & parish-proud
  • From scratch — ranch, slaw, consommé
  • Everywhere — markets, schools, your street
